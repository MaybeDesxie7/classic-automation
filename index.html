Our Products

Industrial Sensors

Reliable sensors for industrial, commercial, and educational applications.

Our Industrial Sensors include a comprehensive range suitable for various industries:

  • Proximity Sensors: Detect objects without contact; used in conveyor systems, security gates, ATMs, and automated production lines.
  • Photoelectric Sensors: Light-based detection for automated doors, inventory tracking, assembly lines, and laboratory experiments.
  • Temperature Sensors: Monitor and control temperatures in industrial ovens, data centers, HVAC systems, and educational labs.
  • Level Sensors: Measure liquids or solids in tanks, silos, water systems, and chemical storage facilities.
  • Encoders: Provide accurate motion feedback for robotics, conveyor systems, and automation in various sectors.
  • Pressure Sensors: Measure hydraulic or pneumatic pressures in manufacturing, automotive testing, and water treatment plants.
  • Flow Sensors: Monitor flow of liquids, gas, or air in chemical plants, laboratories, and irrigation systems.

Engineered for durability, precision, and reliability across harsh industrial, commercial, and educational environments.

PLCs

Programmable logic controllers for automation across industries.

PLCs automate processes in manufacturing, banking, schools, offices, and smart buildings. They control conveyors, elevators, HVAC, lighting, and security systems. Integration with SCADA enables real-time monitoring and remote management, ensuring safety and efficiency.

AC/DC Motors

Powerful motors for industrial, commercial, and educational purposes.

AC/DC motors are reliable for powering conveyors, pumps, fans, robotics, and industrial machinery. AC motors handle large industrial loads, while DC motors are ideal for precise speed control. Durable, energy-efficient, and low-maintenance for any environment.

Servo Drives

Precision drives for controlled and automated systems.

Servo drives provide accurate motion control for robotics, automated machinery, conveyor systems, and laboratory setups. They enhance performance, energy efficiency, and precision for manufacturing, logistics, educational labs, and smart offices.

Variable Frequency Drives

Efficient motor controllers for adjustable speed and energy savings.

VFDs control speed and torque in motors across industrial, commercial, and educational setups. They reduce energy use, improve process stability, extend motor life, and enable smooth start-up for sensitive equipment.

Encoders

High-precision feedback devices for motion and position control.

Encoders provide accurate speed and position feedback for robotics, automated machinery, conveyors, elevators, and laboratory devices. They ensure efficiency, safety, and synchronization across various industries.

Temperature Controllers

Maintain optimal conditions in industrial, commercial, and educational settings.

Temperature controllers regulate heating and cooling in ovens, HVAC systems, labs, chemical tanks, and manufacturing equipment. They maintain process efficiency, equipment longevity, and product quality across all environments.
