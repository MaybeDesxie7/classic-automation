Our Products

Industrial Sensors

Reliable sensors for industrial, commercial, and educational applications.

Our Industrial Sensors include a comprehensive range suitable for various industries, from manufacturing and mining to banks, schools, and research labs:

  • Proximity Sensors: Detect objects without contact; used in conveyor systems, security gates, ATMs, and automated production lines.
  • Photoelectric Sensors: Light-based detection for automated doors, inventory tracking, assembly lines, and laboratory experiments.
  • Temperature Sensors: Monitor and control temperatures in industrial ovens, data centers, HVAC systems, and educational labs.
  • Level Sensors: Measure liquids or solids in tanks, silos, water systems, and chemical storage facilities.
  • Encoders: Provide accurate motion feedback for robotics, conveyor systems, and automation in various sectors.
  • Pressure Sensors: Measure hydraulic or pneumatic pressures in manufacturing, automotive testing, and water treatment plants.
  • Flow Sensors: Monitor flow of liquids, gas, or air in chemical plants, laboratories, and irrigation systems.

These sensors are engineered for durability, high precision, and reliability across harsh industrial environments, office automation, and educational setups.

PLCs

Programmable logic controllers for automation across industries.

PLCs automate processes in manufacturing, mining, banking systems, schools, and smart buildings. They control conveyors, elevators, HVAC systems, lighting, and security mechanisms. For example, in factories, PLCs manage production lines; in schools, they automate lab equipment; in banks, they control secure access systems. Integration with SCADA enables real-time monitoring and remote management.

AC/DC Motors

Powerful motors for industrial, commercial, and educational purposes.

Our AC/DC motors provide reliable performance in diverse industries: powering conveyors in factories, pumps in water treatment plants, fans in office buildings, robotics in educational labs, and crushers or ventilation systems in mining. AC motors suit large industrial loads, while DC motors are ideal for variable speed applications. They offer durability, energy efficiency, and low maintenance across environments.

Servo Drives

Precision drives for controlled and automated systems.

Servo drives enable accurate motion control in automation and robotics across industries including manufacturing, logistics, schools, and smart offices. They optimize machinery performance, improve energy efficiency, and support precision tasks such as CNC machines, robotic arms, conveyor sorting, and lab experiments. Ideal for environments where precise movement and control are critical.

Variable Frequency Drives

Efficient motor controllers for adjustable speed and energy savings.

VFDs control motor speed and torque in diverse applications, from industrial conveyor belts, pumps, and fans to HVAC systems in offices, educational lab equipment, and automated assembly lines. They reduce energy consumption, improve process stability, extend motor life, and provide smooth start-up for sensitive machinery.

Encoders

High-precision feedback devices for motion and position control.

Encoders are essential for precise motion control in robotics, automated machinery, industrial conveyors, elevators, and laboratory devices. They provide accurate speed and position feedback, supporting efficiency, safety, and synchronization in production lines, smart offices, and educational projects.

Temperature Controllers

Maintain optimal conditions in industrial, commercial, and educational settings.

Temperature controllers regulate heating and cooling processes across industries: manufacturing ovens, HVAC systems in offices and schools, laboratory incubators, chemical tanks, and mining equipment. They ensure equipment longevity, process efficiency, and material quality by maintaining precise temperature conditions in any environment.
